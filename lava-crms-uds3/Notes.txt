Installation:
  Put lava-crms-uds3 in your workspace.
  Add the following to your context-<appname>.xml
        <import resource="crms/uds3-context.xml"/>
  build.xml
    Update your build.xml for UTF-8.  Add the following attr to the javac tag:
        encoding='UTF-8'
    Add the following to build.xml under the project tag.
        <property name="lava-crms-uds3.path" value="${workspace.path}/lava-crms-uds3" />
    Add the following to build.xml under the javac tag:
        <src path="${lava-crms-uds3.path}/${src.folder}" />
    Add the following to build.xml under the 'copy' tag:
        <fileset dir="${lava-crms-uds3.path}" excludes="**/CVS/**,database/**,src/**,**/*.class,bin/**,.classpath,.project,build.*" />
